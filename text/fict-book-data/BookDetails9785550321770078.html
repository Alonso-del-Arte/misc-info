The Familiar

by Anna Sersch

Fiction > Fantasy > Wizards

Paperback, $18.99

An immortal cat reminisces about the wizards he served. Since being adopted as a little kitten by a wizard, he has gained experience and a touch of magic through the years. Now, after having served many wizards, he might become a master himself, but a sidestepped problem from his past might cost him everything now.

Critics hail Anna Sersch's début novel as "a magical and whim­si­cal journey, at times terrifying but al­ways managing the tonal shifts with grace and sub­tle­ty." (Ron Troupe, Daily Planet).

First published 2019, reprinted 2020, 2021 and scheduled for reprinting late 2022. ISBN-16: 978-555-0-32177-007-8
