Early Birds Club

by Thomas Thomason

Fiction > Thriller > Spy

Paperback, $18.99

Shadows of the Cold War come to the fore in this spy thriller. MI-6 Agent Michaela McThomp­son­gilly must expose a double agent at Downing Street while also dealing with her gender transition and quelling rumors that her recently deceased father betrayed his handler in Kiev back in 1964.

Critics hail Thomas Thomason's début novel as "a taut thriller packed with twists from beginning to end" (Ron Troupe, Daily Planet).

First published 2019, reprinted 2021. ISBN-16: 978-555-0-32177-034-4
