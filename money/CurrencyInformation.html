* How many digits for subdivisions of the unit. Most currencies are subdivided into 100 cents (hence this column shows 2 for most currencies). In a few cases, a dinar is subdivided into 1,000 darahim (which corresponds to 3 in this column). A few currencies, like the Japanese yen, are not subdivided at all (so there's a 0 in this column). And for precious metals, like gold or silver, and a few other currencies, subdivision is not applicable (hence −1 in this column).
