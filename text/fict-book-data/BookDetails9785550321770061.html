The Path or the Journey

On a quest for the Holy Grail, Lohengrin must face his demons, and realize that only the love of a woman can save him from the catastrophes looming around him.

Critics hail Clayten Bruce's début novel as "a tour de force, mas­ter­ful­ly blending familiar and esoteric elements to produce a compelling narrative that will have you turning the pages as the pot boils." (Cat Grant, Daily Planet).
